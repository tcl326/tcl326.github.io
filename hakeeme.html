Hakeeme

about

A wearable device to monitor construction worker health. Provides anonymized reports of working conditions to governments/third party monitors and can provide warnings to workers if they are getting dehydrated or getting too much sun. The hardware consists of a heartrate monitor, a temperature/humidity sensor, and a Bluetooth module controlled by an Arduino. Currently, the hardware system is under active development by the Dubai Government.
